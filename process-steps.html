Steps for post your property

Login/Register

  • Click on Login\Register up button in the top-right corner
  • Enter your E-mail Id and Password
  • Click on Login
  • If you are a New user then click on Register
  • Fill all the required details and click on Register now
Once you are logged in, you will be redirected to your Dashboard page

In your Dashboard

  • Click on New button in your dashboard page, after clicking that you will be redirected to new form.

In your "Add new Property" form

  • Fill your house Details and contact details
  • uplaod some image of your property

Submit

  • After filling all the details click on Post this property button in form.
  • After clicking on button your property will be added.
